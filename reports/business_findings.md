# Business Findings

## Executive Summary
- Generated and analyzed 18,000 orders covering 2024-01-01 through 2025-12-31.
- Total net revenue reached $29,280,226.12 with $9,347,086.05 in gross profit and a 31.9% profit margin.
- Logistics performance averaged 5.0 delivery days with 70.2% on-time fulfillment and a 7.1% return rate.
- Northeast led all regions with $2,094,482.43 in gross profit at a 33.3% margin.

## Strongest Segments
- Midwest Industrial delivered $739,087.94 in profit on $1,789,659.59 of revenue with a 41.3% margin and 91.9% on-time delivery.
- Northeast Office Supplies delivered $652,037.87 in profit on $1,487,543.41 of revenue with a 43.8% margin and 81.7% on-time delivery.
- Northeast Electronics delivered $499,920.12 in profit on $2,211,197.70 of revenue with a 22.6% margin and 72.8% on-time delivery.

## Underperforming Segments
- Southwest Furniture posted $147,288.40 in profit at a 20.8% margin. Key issues: Low margin; Late deliveries; High returns; Backorders; Slow fulfillment.
- Southeast Electronics posted $404,513.76 in profit at a 15.6% margin. Key issues: Low margin; Late deliveries; High returns; Backorders; Slow fulfillment.
- West Apparel posted $234,096.83 in profit at a 41.3% margin. Key issues: Late deliveries; High returns; Backorders; Slow fulfillment.
- Southeast Furniture posted $177,662.26 in profit at a 28.9% margin. Key issues: Low margin; Backorders; Slow fulfillment.

## Recommended Actions
- Review pricing and discount controls for Southwest Furniture to protect margin leakage.
- Rebalance inventory and carrier capacity for Southwest Furniture to shorten delivery cycles.
- Increase safety stock and supplier lead-time monitoring for Southwest Furniture.
- Reduce returns in Southwest Furniture through packaging, fit, and order-accuracy improvements.
- Review pricing and discount controls for Southeast Electronics to protect margin leakage.
